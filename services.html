Our Comprehensive Diagnostic Services

Bringing advanced and reliable health testing conveniently to you.

Your Health, Our Expertise

At Biotech Diagnostic Services, we offer a wide spectrum of diagnostic tests designed to provide accurate and timely insights into your health. Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with advanced technology, operated by highly trained professionals committed to precision and reliability.

Understanding the demands of modern life, we emphasize convenience and accessibility. Our signature **home sampling service** brings professional sample collection right to your doorstep, saving you valuable time and ensuring comfort. Beyond this, we provide a comprehensive test menu catering to routine check-ups, specialized investigations, and chronic disease management.

We are dedicated to supporting your health journey with dependable results and exceptional service, every step of the way.

Explore Our Test Categories

From routine health screenings to specialized diagnostic panels, our extensive test menu covers all your needs.

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Blood Glucose (Fasting, Random, Post-prandial)
  • Lipid Profile (Cholesterol, Triglycerides)
  • Liver Function Tests (LFTs)

Diabetes Management

  • HbA1c

  • Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT)

Infectious Diseases

Hormonal Assays
